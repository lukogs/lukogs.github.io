About Us

Neermax engineering solution LLP is an engineering consultancy and construction company in India and GCC. We offer total solutions for Building engineering works from design services to project execution right up to handing over to our customers. We specialing in design, build, construct, supply, install, service, upgrade, maintenance of building service Systems & Networks, Utilities and Equipment. Neermax works with energy efficient building services with latest technologies in conformity with international standards of quality, security and safety. We utilize trained and experienced staff for the implementation and delivery of projects with commitment to contract terms, conditions, and preservation to the environment.

MEP

Neermax offer total MEP solutions for Buildings from design to project execution right up to handing over to our customers. We offer turnkey engineering solutions for buildings with latest technology in conformity with international standards of quality, security and safety.


Water Treatment

Every process plant, to operate efficiently and reliably, requires a total water treatment system for make-up water, cooling water, boiler water, condensates and water recovery. No matter your size, market, or application, Neermax have the experience, the technology, and the infrastructure to be your water purification partner. We supply treated pure water to any locations as per client requirements.


Sewage Treatment

We offer technically advanced Sewage Treatment Plant, which is used to treat large amount of sewage effectively. With a combination of several steps, our Sewage Treatment Plants treat the toughest sewage without using much power. Our engineer’s works closely with the clients to provide cost-effective solutions for their specific needs.


Operation and Maintenance

We offer annual maintenance contract service for keeping a building functional and livable. Neermax help to client in improving the performance and rehabilitation of their infrastructure asset, there by extracting greater value and reducing original Maintenance Costs.
