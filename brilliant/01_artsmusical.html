Conference

Do You Need Help Organising Your Company’s Next Conference?

This year you’ve been tasked with organising the company conference and you’ve been asked to make sure its different to last year and all the years before. What do you do? You can start by talking to us we are conference organisers with over 15 years combined experience; offering a flexible approach, cost effective delivery, a hands-on culture and unrivalled service standards.

  • Venue finding – Suggesting a range of perfect venues for your next conference.
  • Event management – Pre event, build and during the live event, we’ll be on hand to ensure it all runs smoothly.
  • Conference production – AV, sound, lighting, design, set and stage.
  • Content – 3D visuals, graphic animation, presentations ,banner design.

Conference Management

Our comprehensive conference management service includes everything from finding the right venue, creating a bespoke online registration website (no more updating excel spreadsheets), managing the delegate registration desk on the day and even printing additional last minute name badges. Our event crew will ensure your schedule is on track. Our in house production team will ensure your content is displayed in an exciting and immersive way to suit your audience and brand.

Small, medium or large, we can ensure your next conference is a huge success.

AV, lighting, Production

We have a variety of event production packages for you to choose from. We can supply striking stage sets, conference lighting, presentation lecterns, PA systems and all the technical items required to present a conference effectively. See more information about our conference production services.

Creating a memorable experience

Our top tip for creating a conference that engages and excites audiences, is to create an experience for the delegates. Depending on the scale of your budget you can create interactive presentations, visual presentations, 3D presentations and much more.

Venue Finding & Management

As one of the UAE’s leading conference organisers, we continually strive to suggest new and exciting venue locations for all our clients. From hotels, conference centres, golf clubs or even marquees, take advantage of our venue finding service.
